About CleanCommute

Chesney, a student from Nairobi, spent hours in traffic daily. She realized the root issue wasn’t just bad roads - but inefficient use of cars. Inspired by cities like Singapore, Copenhagen, and Dubai, she built CleanCommute: a carpool and bike-rental platform for Kenyans who want to save time, money, and the planet.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities to live sustainably by reducing traffic congestion and carbon emissions to promote clean, shared, and efficient urban mobility.

Our Vision

An African cityscape where every ride helps the environment, saves money, and builds community.

How CleanCommute Works

Set Your Destination

Open the app and enter where you’re going. Whether it’s work, class, or a meet-up, we’ll find the greenest way to get you there.

Choose Your Ride

Pick from a smart carpool or a nearby bike. Get matched with trusted commuters heading your way or unlock a bike for solo travel.

Ride & Save

Travel smarter - save on costs, avoid traffic, and cut your carbon footprint. Every ride helps reduce emissions.

Track Your Impact

See how much CO₂ you’ve saved with every trip. Your personal carbon dashboard keeps you motivated and shows your green journey over time.
